## Overview

The discrete cell complex on the periodic lattice $(\mathbb{Z}/N)^d$:
sites (from `gaussian-field`), links (oriented 1-cells = site +
direction), and plaquettes (oriented 2-cells = site + ordered
direction pair). Provides the four-link counterclockwise boundary
of a plaquette (`boundaryLinks`), key combinatorial incidence
lemmas (each link sits in $\leq 2(d-1)$ plaquettes; two distinct
links share $\leq 1$ plaquette when $N \geq 3$), and a separate
2D asymmetric lattice (`AsymLink`, `AsymPlaquette`) used for the
2D Doeblin-path mass-gap proof. Most of the file is the technical
proof of `plaquettes_per_link_le'` (an injection into $(\mathrm{Fin}\,d
\setminus \{\mathrm{dir}\}) \times \mathrm{Bool}$). Reference:
Chatterjee (2026), §15.2 + Appendix C.8.

### [`shared_plaquettes_le_one`](../../../LGT/Lattice/CellComplex.lean#L596) — Theorem

### [`plaquettes_per_link_le'`](../../../LGT/Lattice/CellComplex.lean#L625) — Theorem

**Statement**: Each link $\ell$ lies on at most $2(d-1)$ plaquettes. Proof: define an injection $f : \{\text{plaquettes containing } \ell\} \hookrightarrow (\mathrm{Fin}\,d \setminus \{\ell.\mathrm{dir}\}) \times \mathrm{Bool}$ sending $p \mapsto (\text{the other direction in }\{\mathrm{dir1}, \mathrm{dir2}\}, \, [p.\mathrm{site} = \ell.\mathrm{site}])$. Cardinality: $|\mathrm{Fin}\,d \setminus \{\cdot\}| \cdot |\mathrm{Bool}| = (d-1) \cdot 2$. The injectivity verification is the bulk of the file (~120 lines): four cases on whether $\ell.\mathrm{dir}$ matches each plaquette's `dir1` or `dir2`, with two of the four cases ruled out by the strict order $\mathrm{dir1} < \mathrm{dir2}$.

**Proof uses**: `boundaryLink_dir_cases'` (private), `siteShift_injective`, `plaquette_ext'` (private), `Finset.card_image_of_injOn`, `Finset.card_le_card`, `Finset.card_product`, `Finset.card_erase_of_mem`
