## Overview

Encodes the Wilson lattice gauge theory as a `GibbsSpec` on the
link-indexed configuration space, with sites = links and spin space =
the compact gauge group $G$. The conditional distribution
$\gamma(\Lambda, \sigma)$ on $\Lambda \subset \mathrm{links}$ given
boundary $\sigma$ on $\Lambda^c$ is the pushforward of product Haar
through the glue map $u_\Lambda \mapsto \mathrm{glue}_\Lambda(u_\Lambda, \sigma)$,
re-weighted by $w/Z_\Lambda(\sigma)$. Verifies the four `GibbsSpec`
axioms — normalization, consistency, properness, measurable
$\sigma \mapsto \gamma(\Lambda, \sigma)(A)$ — and constructs `ymGibbsSpec`
which is the bridge to upstream Dobrushin uniqueness machinery.
