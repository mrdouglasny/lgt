## Overview

Defines the finite-volume Yang-Mills probability measure on gauge field
configurations $G^{\mathrm{links}}$ for a compact group $G$ and a finite
lattice. Builds: normalized Haar probability $\mu_G$ on $G$, product Haar
$\mu_{\mathrm{Haar}}$ on configurations, the Wilson Boltzmann weight
$w(U) = \exp(-S(U))$, the partition function $Z = \int w\,d\mu_{\mathrm{Haar}}$
(proved $> 0$), the Yang-Mills measure $\mu_{\mathrm{YM}} = (1/Z)w \cdot
\mu_{\mathrm{Haar}}$ as an honest `Measure`, and the bridge
$\mathrm{ymExpect}(f) = \int f\,d\mu_{\mathrm{YM}}$. Reference:
Chatterjee (2026), §15.3–15.4.

### [`partitionFn_pos`](../../../LGT/MassGap/YMMeasure.lean#L143) — Theorem

**Statement**: $Z > 0$ at strong coupling. Lower-bound the integrand uniformly: for every $U$, $w(U) \geq c := e^{-\beta \cdot |\mathrm{plaq}| \cdot 2n}$ (worst case $S(U) \leq \beta \cdot |\mathrm{plaq}| \cdot 2n$). Integrating against the probability measure gives $Z \geq c > 0$.
