### [`ym_mass_gap_2pt_via_multisite`](../../../LGT/MassGap/MassGap3D.lean#L257) — Theorem

**Statement**: The genuine **distance-parameterized YM multisite covariance bound**. For U(n) Wilson lattice gauge theory at strong coupling, given (i) the standard analytic discharges (conditional partition function positivity, Boltzmann measurability, conditional integrability/measurability for the DLR identity), (ii) plaquette-observable integrability, (iii) the influence bound `hInfluence` (off-plaquette → 0) and neighbor-count bounds `hMaxNeighbors{Col,Row}`, (iv) the condKernel a.e. bound `hcond_ae_bound`, (v) any link distance `dLink` with refl, triangle, and nearest-neighbor support, plus finite-support and cylinder-set local-dependence witnesses, conclude:
$$\bigl|\mathrm{conn2pt}(O_p, O_q)\bigr| \leq 2n^2 \sum_{x \in \partial p}\sum_{y \in \partial q} \frac{\alpha^{\mathrm{dLink}(y,x)}}{1 - \alpha},$$
where $\alpha = \mathrm{dobrushinAlpha}(n, d, \beta)$ and $\partial p, \partial q$ are the four-link boundaries. This is the wrapper that `ym_mass_gap_strong_coupling` in `StrongCoupling.lean` discharges hypotheses for, and that `ym_mass_gap_exponential_decay` ultimately invokes (with `dLink` the ambient shared-plaquette graph distance, per the completion plan).
