covariance bound from `markov-semigroups`. Takes the YM Wilson lattice
gauge theory at strong coupling and a generic link distance
$d_{\mathrm{link}}$ (with reflexivity, triangle inequality, and
nearest-neighbor support), discharges ~20 measurability/integrability/
positivity hypotheses from a single continuity input
$\mathrm{Continuous}\,\rho$ and the trace bounds
$\lvert \mathrm{Re}\,\mathrm{tr}(g) \rvert \leq n$, and produces the
multisite covariance bound
$|\mathrm{conn2pt}| \leq 2 n^2 \sum_{x \in \partial p, y \in \partial q}

**target theorem** `ym_mass_gap_exponential_decay` (one remaining
sorry, route in `docs/mass-gap-completion-plan.md`) and the L¹
periodic torus distance scaffolding the eventual proof will use.

## The strong coupling wrapper theorem

### [`ym_mass_gap_strong_coupling`](../../../LGT/MassGap/StrongCoupling.lean#L1587) — Theorem

**Statement**: **The headline result of this file.** Distance-parameterized hypothesis-discharging wrapper. Inputs:
- $G$ a compact gauge group with `HasGaugeTrace`, `HasHaarProbability`, `Continuous (HasGaugeTrace.rep)`.
- Standard YM data ($d, N, \beta$, plaq, $p, q$, trace bounds, strong-coupling).
- A link distance $d_{\mathrm{link}}$ with reflexivity, triangle inequality, and nearest-neighbor support against $\gamma_{\mathrm{YM}}$.

$$\bigl|\mathrm{conn2pt}\,(O_p, O_q)\bigr| \leq 2n \cdot n \sum_{x \in \partial p}\sum_{y \in \partial q} \frac{\alpha^{d_{\mathrm{link}}(y,x)}}{1-\alpha},$$

### [`ym_mass_gap_exponential_decay`](../../../LGT/MassGap/StrongCoupling.lean#L1941) — Theorem

**Statement**: **The mass-gap target.** For U(n) Wilson lattice gauge theory on $(\mathbb{Z}/N)^d$ with $d \geq 2$, $N \geq 3$, $n \geq 1$, $\beta < \tfrac{1}{32n(d-1)}$:
$$\bigl|\mathrm{conn2pt}\,(O_p, O_q)\bigr| \leq \frac{32 n^2}{1 - \alpha} \cdot \alpha^{(\mathrm{latticePlaquetteDist}\,p\,q - 1) / 2},$$
where $\alpha = \mathrm{dobrushinAlpha}(n, d, \beta) < 1$ and the exponent uses Nat subtraction and division (saturating at 0 for close-range plaquettes). The factor of $1/2$ in the exponent is forced by the geometry (one shared-plaquette influence-graph step displaces a link anchor by up to 2 L¹ site-units). **Status: open** (one sorry); route in [`docs/mass-gap-completion-plan.md`](../../../docs/mass-gap-completion-plan.md): instantiate `ym_mass_gap_strong_coupling` at the ambient shared-plaquette graph distance on links, then bound the 16-term boundary-link sum via the boundary-layer incidence geometry.
